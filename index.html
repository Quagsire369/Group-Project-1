Ingredients On Hand

    Welcome to "What's on Hand"

    "What's on Hand" is a website for cocktail enthusiasts like yourself! We're not just about mixing drinks; we've got a little something extra to make your experience even more memorable. Let us introduce you to our ice breakers. Say goodbye to awkward silences at parties and hello to fascinating conversations!

    Shake things up with random facts:
    Have you ever been at a party and found yourself running out of interesting things to talk about? Fear not! We've got you covered with our handy-dandy random fact generator. Give it a spin, simply click the button in the right column. You'll become the life of the party in no time!

    Now, let's move on to the cocktail search. Here's a refresher on how to find your perfect drink:

    Add some ingredients:
    On the left side, you'll find a convenient search field where you can enter the ingredients you have on hand. Click the "Add" button to include them in your list. If you want to remove an item, simply click the black remove button next to the ingredient. Need a fresh start? Hit the "Clear" button to clear all your ingredients.

    Show possible cocktails:
    Once you've added your ingredients, click the "Show Drinks" button, and a list of possible drinks will populate the center column.

    Dive into the recipes:
    Curious about a specific drink? Simply click on it, and let the recipe and instructions unfold. Every detail you need to concoct the perfect drink is right there.

    Search with precision:
    Craving a particular drink? Use our trusty search bar. Type in the name of the drink you desire and click "Search." In an instant, a list of matching results will appear.

    Save your favorites:
    Found a drink that you want to remember? Expand its recipe by clicking on it. To ensure you can enjoy it over and over again, click the pulsing "+" button. It will be added to your "My Cocktails" list, nestled in the upper right-hand corner of the page. Access it anytime by clicking the menu icon.

    Now you're all set to discover and make your favorite cocktails, armed with intriguing ice breakers and a knack for mixing drinks. Get ready to impress, engage, and sip on the finest creations. Cheers to great drinks, good times, and captivating conversations!
